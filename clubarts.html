Student Clubs - Arts

FALCONS
Falcons VIT
Being the oldest aero design team of VIT build aircrafts of various sizes and functionality and take part in various international and national competitions. We work with a pioneering spirit that puts innovation at the heart of everything we do. Primarily taking part in the SAE Aero Design competition in the regular class, assailing falcons is known for its highly radical and unique designs of aircrafts. In an endeavour to outdo ourselves every year we put our heart and soul in every aspect of our aircraft, starting from research to design and analysis to fabrication and testing.
Kshatriya
Team Kshatriya
TeamKshatriya was formed in the year 2006 , by B.Tech Mechanical Engineering students of VIT.We started with the objective of bringing together those brains which are diligently working on a myriad of inventive technologies, and to provide them with a forum for free exchange of ideas and expose them to the latest innovations in terms of automotive engineering.The most important part is we have knowledge and lessons learnt from mistakes passed on from senior teams to junior teams. This is what makes us unique! Today, we are gearing up for BAJA SAE INDIA 2016 and looking forward to the challenge ahead! With us, EXPERIENCE ENGINEERING!
Pravega
Pravega Racing
Pravega Racing is the official Formula Student Combustion team of this University. We take part in Formula Student competitions held all around the world under the aegis of Society of Automotive Engineers ( SAE International).
We have participated in competitions in California(2010), Italy(2011, 2012, 2013) , Germany(2014) and the first ever JK Tyre Formula Design Challenge(2015), Coimbatore; wherein we achieved 3rd position overall in the country. We will be one of the select few Indian teams which will participate in Formula Student Germany 2015. We strive to make our country proud and bring laurels to our University.
Vimaanas
Team Vimaanas
Team Vimaanas is one of the most Consistently performing International Teams in VIT; specializing in the Design and Fabrication of Highly Efficient Light Weight Portable Remote Controlled Air Planes. Our team is a mix of Aero-Modelling Enthusiasts, Technical Experts and neatly suited up Managers all molded into one Perfect Team. Vimaanas has the onus of producing the best finishes in the SAE Aero Design competition every year.
We believe in putting our best foot forward in any task presented and working seamlessly as a family we call “Team Vimaanas”.

Gravitas
Riviera is VIT University’s ISO certified Annual International Sports and Cultural Carnival. This festival is an endeavour to provide a platform to all individuals to showcase their skills and develop their potential via arts, sports, music, dance, drama, debate or fashion. The four day extravaganza sees participation of students from diverse backgrounds, nationalities and cultures.
