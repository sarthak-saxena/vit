Nano Technology

Funding:

The Nanotechnology Laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art nano-metrology, nanomanipulation and characterization facilities. These include Advanced Optical Microscope, Advanced Atomic Force (AFM) Microscope, Scanning Tunnelling Microscope (STM) and Scanning Probe Microscope (SPM), Electrochemical Workstation with many advanced features for understanding and characterization of nanomateric structures, building and characterizing specialized devices. The above facilities to the tune of approximately Rs. 86 lakhs were provided by VIT.

The center has received a grant of Rs 2.94 Crores from DST, Government of India under Nano Science and Technology Initiative (NSTI) for the Postgraduate Teaching Program (M. Tech.). A new wet Chemical Synthesis Lab has been constructed, housing 5-digit accuracy Weighing Balance, High-speed Centrifuge, Rotaevaporator, Spin-Coater, Fume-Hood, Chemical Work-Bench, Distill water plant, Thin Film Metal Evaporation and CVD facility under this project. A Class 1000 Clean Room is in place with provisions for a Lithographic facility and Keithley Semiconductor Parameter Analyzer also to be housed.
