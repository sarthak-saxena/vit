Community Outreach (CSRD & RS )

A thirst for serving the rural needs and challenges saw its birth on Dec 1st 2005, as the Centre for Sustainable Rural Development and Research Studies (CSRD&RS), with the visionary support of our Chancellor Dr.G.VISWANATHAN. It was inaugurated by His Excellency Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam, President of India on 1st December, 2005 to provide better and effective services for the growth and development of rural needy through its various activities. It sees through the sustainable development and working among the socially-economically deprived and marginalized communities including children, youth, women, farmers in the community. It provides for better and effective services for the growth and development of rural needy through its various activities and projects.

The aim of CSRD & RS is to improve the quality of the life of the rural folks by meeting their basic needs of the present without compromising the ability of the future generations through participative planning and implementation.

Mission: Promoting sustainable rural development through participative planning, capacity building , effective networking, Research and innovative efforts.

"Go to the People. Live among them. Learn from them. Serve them. Plan with them. Start with what they know. Build on what they have. And when the best leaders leave, the people will say, "We have done it ourselves." -Lao Tsu
