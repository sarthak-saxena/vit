FACULTY HONORS
  • Prof. (Dr.) J. P. Raina has been appointed as Emeritus Professor at CNR. He is a reviewer for IET-Circuits, devices and systems and Optik, Elsevier.
  • Dr. A. Nirmala Grace was invited as a postdoctoral/Senior researcher at Korea Institute of Energy Research (KIER), Daejeon, South Korea in 2013.
  • Dr. Gargi Raina is a reviewer for Elsevier and Springer publications. Dr. A. Nirmala Grace has reviewed more than 30 papers from different publishers namely, Elsevier, Springer, Royal Society of Chemistry and American Chemical Society. Mr. V. Velmurugan is a reviewer of journals from Elsevier publications. Mr. N. K. Sahu has been reviewing journals from American Institute of Physics publishers.
  • Dr. Gargi Raina is an Editorial board member of Nano Science and Technology Consortium (NTSC), New Delhi. Dr. A. Nirmala Grace is on the Editorial board of journals such as Nanosciences and Nanotechnologies: An International Journal (NIJ), Graphene, Frontiers in Polymer Chemistry, Advances in Nanoparticles, Advances in Materials Physics and Chemistry and World Journal of Nano Science and Engineering. Dr. Anita S. Ethiraj has been appointed as a member of ‘Technical Advisory Board’ for all affiliated Journals of Blue Eyes Intelligence Engineering & Sciences Publication Pvt. Ltd. (BEI-ESP).
  • Dr. A. Nirmala Grace was invited to be one of the panel members for evaluating Indo-Russia projects 2014 from Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Govt. of India.
  • Dr. Anita S. Ethiraj is invited as a member of the Board of Studies for PG Program with M.Tech Nanotechnology, M. Tech Nano Electronics and Photonics at Centre for Nano Science and Technology, IST, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad. She was also selected for the University Grants Commission (UGC) funded NRC-M workshop on ‘Biomaterials’ at Department of Materials Engineering, Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore
STUDENTS HONORS
  • Four students of M. Tech. Nanotechnology Batch 2011-13 were selected for Doctoral programs at premier institutes in India and abroad.
  • An outstanding student achievement of MAREPALLY BHANUCHANDRA of this batch, was being one among NINE students selected worldwide for the PhD–ERASMUS-MUNDUS Fellowship for SINCHEM for the year 2013-14. He was the only candidate selected from India. He also received the honor of being elected as Program representative of SINCHEM.
LAB FACILITY EXPANSION
  • The Nanotechnology lab has procured new facilities such as Nanofluid Interferometer, Naio AFM with advanced imaging techniques such as MFM, EFM and lithography under VIT and DST Nanomission PG program.
RESEARCH OUTPUT
  • The center has 37 publications in the year 2013-14 in peer-reviewed international journals with a cumulative impact factor of 65.275.
FUNDED PROJECTS
  • M. Tech Nanotechnology program received ONE Year extension in Oct 2013 under the DST Nanomission PG program with the release of Rs. 40 Lakhs.
  • Dr. A. Nirmala Grace and Mr. V. Velmurugan received a project from DRDO-CVRDE to the tune of Rs. 24.3 Lakhs in 2013, entitled "Development of Engine Nanocoolants for Armoured Fighting Vehicles".
