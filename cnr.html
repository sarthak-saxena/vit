Nano Technology

Objectives:
  • To support academic and research programs in nanotechnology with specific emphasis on nano-electronics and nano-Photonics.
  • To evolve various nanofabrication techniques such as nanolithography and nanopatterning for creating functional nanostructures and devices.
  • To synthesize new nano structures employing physical, chemical and biological methods.
  • Characterize the various nano-structures and qualitatively and quantitatively assess the materials and devices.
  • To develop novel and functional nanomaterials for applications towards fuel cells, sensors, bio-medical applications and Opto-electronic materials.
  • To study various quantum phenomena of structures and transport mechanisms at nanometric and sub-nanometric scales.
  • To develop simulation tools for various transport phenomena and device modelling at the nanoscale.
  • To build optical interference lithographic techniques at UV and at EUV.
  • To undertake consultancy programs for industry and provide requisite technological support.
  • To provide support for the industry in interdisciplinary domains of nanotechnology.
  • To study molecular electronics and spintronics systems and bio-electronic systems.
  • To study self-assembly techniques through chemical and biological roots.
  • To disseminate information through workshops, seminars, symposia, and National and International conferences.

Goals:
To build competencies in following areas of urgent need for the advancement of knowledge.
  • Synthesis of various active nano-electronic and nano-photonic devices
  • Evolve self-assembly techniques for the fabrication of such devices and structures through chemical and biological route
  • Build Optical Interference Lithographic Techniques
  • Evolve techniques for simulation and modelling of SETs and Quantum Dots and build the devices with the available infrastructure
  • Study various quantum phenomena of such structures and transport mechanisms of nano-metric and sub-nano-metric scales
  • Provide support for the industry in interdisciplinary domains of nanotechnology
