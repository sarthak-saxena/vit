Our Vision:
We, at Centre for Nanobiotechnology, VIT University will carry out cutting edge research of global standards through our dedicated faculty and students, and apply the acquired scientific knowledge towards overall improvement of human life.

Mission:
Our mission is to ignite young minds from across the globe thorough sparks of cutting edge knowledge. With great commitment and endurance, we will always support the scholars with innovative thoughts and ideas for effective applied research.
