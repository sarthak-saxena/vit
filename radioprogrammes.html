Community Radio | Programmes

VIT CR runs both on-air and off-air programs. The off-air programs focus on community development initiatives through workshops, school competitions and awareness programs. The one-air programs are organized into 3 phases. Phase I produce programmes with VIT Teamspeak, a club from VIT CR, other Clubs & Chapters. Phase II produce programmes with Schools & Colleges around Vellore. Phase III produce programmes with community people. The idea behind this working model is to produce programmes of different quality and content. Phase I programmes are produced by VIT students for themselves. Phase II programmes are produced by other school and college students for themselves. Phase III programmes are produced by community people for themselves.

Not only in Tamil and English, VIT CR is producing programmes in Telugu and Hindi. VIT CR is sharing its programmes to other Community Radio Stations through internet and also sharing via EK Duniya Anak Awaz, a programme sharing web platform conceptualized and powered by One World South Asia, and supported by Commonwealth Educational Media Centre for Asia (CEMCA).

VIT CR broadcast “BBC Hour”, daily special programmes from 12 noon to 1 pm and 7 pm to 8 pm offered by British Broadcasting Corporation.
On Air Programmes:
Tamil Programmes
  • Ariviyal Aayiram

    This is a informal programme packed with chats and information with an experts.

  • Anubava Medai

    Every Episode of Anubava Medai will portrait an ordinary men and women life from their perspective. This is an interview based programme.

  • Dhinam Oru Thiravukool

    This programme will introduce a new way to observe our world from an expert point of view. Dr. Tamilselvan, Mrs. Jagadha Tamilselvan, Mr. Ramasamy are the experts who are all provide various information for the empowerment of the community in daily basis.

  • Karasaram

    This debate programme is exploring the new wave of thoughts from student community. Each episode will carry an interesting title which will be useful for them and society.

  • Karuthukalam

    It is Vox Populi from VIT Community Radio. This platform provides an opportunity to the people to expose their ideas and thoughts on many more subjects, issues.

  • Kathakelu Kathakelu

    This is a story narrating programme for kids.

  • Kavithai Kelungal

    This art section programme provides a space for the young poem writers in Tamil language. The original poems from the talents decorate this 5 minutes chunk.

  • Maruthuva Neram

    It is an Interview series which take interviews from Doctors and Consultants of various medical areas.

  • Pallikodam

    This programme introduces more new voices from the schools of Vellore, particularly, primary school students.

  • Pudhirodu Vilaiyadu

    This is a game show, which contains more puzzles and riddles for the students to explore Mathematics and brain games.

  • Sevichelvam

    This Tamil programme attempts to create a full-fledged encyclopedia in audio format.

  • Thamizhsudar

    This art section programme connects the Tamil Sangam Literature and current social events, issues and lifestyle.

  • Thiraikadaloodi

    This is a travelogue in VIT Community Radio which covers special locations from different views in Tamil.

  • Valunar Paarvai

    An Interview series from the experts of various fields. This programme will give more information from the particular field, which will be understood by a common man and woman.

    3. Riviera 2015: Riviera Beats, a student team of Anchors, Coordinators, Managers was formed exclusively for Riviera 2015. Live Expos, Live promotional programmes and Live Programmes in VIT Community Radio were done.
    4. Promoted Gravitas 2013 with the special test transmission for six days (24th September 2013 to 29th September 2013) including three days of live programmes. Live Outdoor Broadcast Bytes were done from Gravitas events arena across our university.Gravitas 2014 special promotional programmes and live programmes are also done.
    5. VIT Teamspeak conducted an audition for its five sections including Tamil, English, Telugu, Hindi and Technical Sections from 05th – 7th March, 2014.
    6. VIT CR providing more space for other clubs of VIT including Film Society, E2PC, Dramatics, Tamil Literary Association, Music Club and so on.
    7. VIT CR supporting the students who are participating in Short Films competitions by dubbing and song production.
  • Workshops and training

      Organized the Summer Training Camp 2013 for 20 VIT students and imparted training for their programme production.

      An Extra Curricular Course for 100 students offered by VIT Community Radio every year. Theory Classes, Training programmes, workshops were conducted to train the students with various skills required for community radio broadcast.

      As a part of Dream Camp, a nationwide popular activity by Make A Difference Club (MAD), VIT Community Radio arranged a studio visit for 25 school students on 16th March, 2014. Recording, Transmission, Programming processes and technology, equipment usage were explained during this visit.
  • School Competitions

      Teachers Day & International Literacy Day Competitions for School Students in different categories. Talk, Song, Drama and Quiz competitions both in English and Tamil were conducted on 30th and 31st of August 2014. These competitions were recorded for broadcast on Teachers day 5th September 2014. The rebroadcast was done on 7th September 2014.
      In Total, 122 students from 12 schools around Vellore were participated in these competitions. The questions asked in the Quiz competitions were picked from the life and achievements of Dr. Sarvapalli Radhakrishnan and our education system. In the English Quiz, Vidhya Niketan Matriculation Hr. Sec. School won the first place, Vision Vidhyashram won the second place and Ida Scudder School won the third place. In Tamil Quiz, Vedhavalli School, Walaja won the first place, Kings Matriculation Hr. Sec. School won the second place and St. Marks Matriculation Hr. Sec. School won the third place
  • College Competetions

    To celebrate VIT CR’s First Anniversary, VIT CR conducted a college level radio competition in December 2014. Students were participated in three different categories such as Radio Jockeying, Original Song, Drama.
