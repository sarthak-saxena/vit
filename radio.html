VIT Community Radio

VIT Community Radio 90.8 MHz, is an initiative by the university started for the empowerment of the Vellore community.
Our vision is to provide an opportunity to the community and our students, to get to know their basic rights, duties and responsibilities as citizens in order to be successful in life through our programmes, interactions, events and activities.
Our mission is to build an effective, vibrant and sustainable community, to support development in health, education, environment, agriculture, rural and all other aspects.

Our full Time Transmission (6 Hours per Day) was started with a Fixed Point Chart on 14th April, 2014.

VIT CR has a full-fledged acoustic studio for recording and live programmes. The station is situated in Silver Jubilee Tower (SJT). The studio is facilitated with a recording studio with talkback, transmission booth for transmission control and editing section for post-production.
