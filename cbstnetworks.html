Centre for Bio Separation Technology (CBST)

National & International Networks

Prof. M.A. Vijayalakshmi has an extensive National and International Networks and Collaborative Programmes in terms of Students/ Junior & Senior Scientists exchange and trainings. As such about 90 persons from 36 different countries have thus benefited from this type of exchange/ training programmes in her Laboratory, UTC, France, for periods varying from one month to five years. Under different exchange programmes, she sends her pupils regularly to many different research labs at the national and international level for short term trainings. She has sent already 3 CBST and 2 VIT University members to UTC, France and 2 more members to USA for different training programs.
