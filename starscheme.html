Star Scheme

VIT has taken new initiative of STARS which aims to provide opportunities to pursue quality higher education to the underprivileged rural students. The Father of our nation, Mahatma Gandhi had rightly said that the soul of India lies in its Villages.VIT University believes that financial roadblocks must never impede opportunities to academic excellence and success. The STARS (Support The Advancement of Rural Students) program is an initiative driven by this very belief in the year 2008. Each year, sixty-two students are chosen from rural Tamil Nadu, each of these students are stars in their own right, having topped the higher secondary examinations conducted by the State Government in their respective districts. Initially this scheme was started in Seven Districts namely Cuddalore, Krishnagiri, Dharmapuri, Thiruvannamalai, Kancheepuram, Villupuram and Vellore. Later STARS scheme was extended to all 31 Districts except in Chennai from 2009 onwards.
