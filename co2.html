Carbon Dioxide and Green Technologies Centre

VISION:
To develop processes and technologies for better utilization of carbon dioxide and meet the global need of renewable fuels using agro materials as feed stock.

To optimize the process parameters for the absorption of carbon dioxide from exhaust gases in the laboratory and then scale it up into industrial scale with proper selection of equipment and development of technologies. The process parameters will be optimized and design parameters will be evaluated for getting better economics using the technologies develop in the centre.

Development of carbon dioxide based diesel fuels and using in diesel engine to get better combustion efficiency and thus reduce the carbon dioxide and other green house gas emission.

Develop a liquid carbon dioxide based fuel which can be used in IC engine for generating power and also as fuel in transport sector.

Develop a carbon dioxide based rotary engine and hybridize with diesel engine for driving vehicles.

Improve the photo synthesis with soil less cultivation and carbon dioxide injection thus reduces the carbon dioxide level in the atmosphere. And also achieve higher productivity per unit area.

Development of a continuous algae reactor with controlled injection of carbon dioxide and improve the yield of algae by reducing the growth period by around 50%.

Development of processes for direct conversion of carbon dioxide to petroleum products.

Objectives (Green Technologies):
  • Developing a new strain of high lipid containing oil seeds like Sterculia to produce more oil per unit area.
  • Cultivating high yielding lipid containing oil seeds like `fish tail palm’ to produce more oil per unit area.
  • Elicitation and characterization of Free Fatty acids present in the seeds of the Non-Edible Vegetable Plants.
  • Selecting potential lipid producing microbes to produce more oil per unit area.
  • Bio-remediation of several heavy metal contaminated sites using microbial and chemical resources.
  • Development of low cost processes for hydrogenation of unsaturated fatty acids and lipids to get better storage life for vegetables oils and bio diesel.
  • Optimization of the Bio diesel production process using oil from Sterculia seeds, ‘fish tail palm’ seeds and other non edible oil seeds.
  • Improve the performance of bio diesel blended fuel with small additive for getting high mechanical and thermal efficiency and also improve the lubricant and also improve the shelf life of lubricant used in IC engines.
  • Study the performance characteristics of SI engine (petrol engine) using the new fuel called BE-A fuel developed in the centre for replacing the petrol either as a pure fuel (100%) or blended fuel (with petrol) and optimize the engine parameters.
  • Develop process for production of BE-A fuel using agro waste and optimize the technology for better economics.
  • Development of process and technology for production of Bio gas from Mango peel, withered flowers, kitchen waste etc and optimize the process condition suing laboratory reactor and then bench scale reactor.
  • Optimize the process condition and technology in pilot plant level for the production of bio diesel using agro waste like paddy straw, cotton stalk, sugar cane thrash etc.
  • Optimization of process and technology and collection of design data for the drying of agro waste like paddy straw, cotton stalk and food grains using solar tunnel dryer.
  • Performance study of the Bio oil as a pure fuel and as blended fuel in DG set for using bio oil as fuel for generating power.
  • Evaluation of energy conservation using LED light in place of conventional tube light, incandescent light and CFL.
  • Installing solar PV waste power plant for generating power for supply to hostels and the academic blocks.
  • Generation for power using slow Pyrolysis of wood with moving bed reactor and optimizing conditions and economics.
  • Installation of solar of concentrator and flat plate collectors for heating the waste/ thermic fluid for supplying the hot water to the bathrooms and kitchen.
  • Development of a hydrogen storage and dispensing system using metal hydride and water for supplying hydrogen to fuel cell bus being developed by ISRO and TATA motors.
