Nano Technology

About M. Tech Nanotechnology

The M.Tech (Nanotechnology) course offered by School of Electronics Engineering at VIT University, Vellore is a specialized course which aims at giving its students a sound understanding of the broad area of nanotechnology encompassing synthesis of nanomaterials, structural property of nanostructures, quantum mechanics and applications. Special emphasis is given to Nanoelectronics and Nanophotonics which are the most promising and emerging areas with a promising future. So far, seven batches with a total number of 188 students have graduated.

In the Indian scenario, nanotechnology based industries are still in their infancy. Students are also trained to carry out high tech research and excel as scientists. As a result, to large extent students completing this course can take up a career in scientific research in organizations ranging from world renowned research labs / universities and industrial R & D centers of MNCs. They are also sought after by the very good Educational Institutions for teaching programs.

The curriculum is designed such that they givea thorough backgroundinthe fundamentals of nanotechnology in the initial 15 month period and in the latter 9 months they carry out research oriented projects. The students gain hands-on experience in using sophisticated equipment such as AFM, STM, SPM and nanomaterial synthesis, lithographic patterning, device modelling and simulation are part of the curriculum which helps them in the overall development. (For details of the courses, visit: http://vit.ac.in/Academics/schools/1/7). This background helps our students to get coveted opportunities in foreign and premier Indian universities and research institutions.
